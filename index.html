When It’s Time to Upgrade: Signs Your Suspension Needs Help

If your 4Runner’s ride feels rough, bouncy, or uneven, it may be time to consider replacing your suspension. Common signs include:

  • Uneven tire wear
  • Excessive nose-dive during braking
  • Clunking or rattling sounds over bumps
  • Sagging rear end when loaded
  • Poor cornering stability
